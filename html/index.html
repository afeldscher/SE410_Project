The Network Community for Software Engineering Education

EdCyan - Software Engineering is taught at universities all across the world. Organizations such as ABET, Software Engineering Body of Knowledge (SWEBOK), and IEEE impose standards that help to govern software engineering curriculum; with the goal being to advance the field as a whole and improve education globally.

One of the most important parts of education are the course materials. These can be anything from assignments, to in class exercises that are designed to improve student learning and understanding. Rather than hoarding materials and keeping them secret, a number of universities have elected to share some of their resources publicly.

The primary purpose of EdCyan is to provide a platform to upload informational materials and share it with others publicly. This allows other programs to build off of what has already been done and spend more time contributing new ideas to the cause.
